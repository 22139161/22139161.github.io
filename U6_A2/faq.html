Frequently Asked Questions

What is Celtec Games?

Celtec Games is a game development company creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences. Our goal is to innovate and deliver fun to our players worldwide.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support by visiting our Contact Us page. You can also email us at help@celtecgames.com or call +44 7457 224119.

Where can I purchase your games?

Our games are available for purchase on various platforms such as Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, and more. Please visit our Products page for more details.

What platforms do your games support?

We develop games for PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and mobile devices. Please check the system requirements on the product pages for specific platform details.

How do I report a bug in a game?

If you encounter a bug, please visit our Contact Us page to report it. You can also reach out to us via email at help@celtecgames.com.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

Refunds are processed through the platform where you made the purchase (e.g., Steam, PlayStation Store). Please review the respective platform's refund policy for more information.
