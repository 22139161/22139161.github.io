Phantom Strike: A Stealth-Action Game Like No Other

The shadows are alive, and they belong to you. I’m excited to finally pull back the curtain on my latest project: Phantom Strike, a tactical stealth-action game where every move could mean the difference between a flawless execution and a deadly confrontation.

The Essence of Phantom Strike

In Phantom Strike, you step into the role of a ghost operative—an elite agent trained to infiltrate, eliminate, and disappear. The game blends methodical stealth with high-stakes combat, giving players the freedom to approach each mission with precision or ruthless efficiency. Whether you prefer silent takedowns in the dark or orchestrating chaos with calculated strikes, the choice is yours.

Core Mechanics and Gameplay

The heart of Phantom Strike lies in its dynamic systems. Light and sound play critical roles—stay in the darkness to remain unseen, or use distractions to manipulate enemy patrols. The game rewards patience but doesn’t punish aggression; if stealth fails, you’ll need to adapt quickly, using a mix of firearms, melee combat, and environmental traps to survive.

Advanced AI ensures that enemies learn from your tactics. Guards will investigate suspicious activity, call for reinforcements, and adapt their strategies based on your previous actions. No two playthroughs will feel exactly the same.

BlackSite: Retribution - The Tactical Shooter That Plays for Keeps

This isn't another run-and-gun power fantasy. BlackSite: Retribution is a brutal, methodical tactical shooter where every decision carries weight and every mistake carries consequences. Set in the shadowy world of deniable operations, you'll lead a team of disavowed operatives through high-stakes missions where the only extraction plan is the one you make yourself.

Core Combat Experience

Ballistics matter here - bullets penetrate different materials realistically, suppression effects blur your vision, and wounds impact your mobility. The AI doesn't cheat, but it doesn't need to - enemies communicate, flank intelligently, and exploit any weakness in your position. Your arsenal is fully customizable but deliberately limited - you'll need to manage every round in your magazine and every piece of equipment in your loadout.

A Campaign Without Heroes

The single-player narrative drops you into a chain of covert operations across global black sites, each mission revealing another layer of a conspiracy that reaches higher than you imagined. There are no good guys here - just professionals trying to complete objectives and maybe, if they're lucky, survive the aftermath. Expect morally ambiguous choices that affect both your squad's loyalty and mission outcomes.

Currently in development, BlackSite: Retribution aims to bring back the tension and tactical depth missing from modern shooters. This is a game where smart play beats fast reflexes, where planning matters more than twitch skills, and where survival is the only victory condition that counts.
