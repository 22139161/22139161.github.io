Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Wrexham, Celtec Games is a dynamic British studio specialising in high-octane first-person shooter (FPS) games. We live and breathe fast-paced action, crafting intense, adrenaline-fueled experiences with tight gunplay, strategic combat, and immersive worlds. Our passion lies in pushing FPS boundaries—whether through gripping single-player campaigns, competitive multiplayer, or innovative co-op modes. Powered by a team of veteran developers and fresh talent, we combine cutting-edge tech with a deep love for the genre. From tactical firefights to all-out warfare, every Celtec game is built to deliver precision, excitement, and replayability. Lock and load—the future of FPS starts here. Ready for the fight?
